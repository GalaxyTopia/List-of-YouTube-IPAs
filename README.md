# List-of-YouTube-IPAs
A dedicated list for finding YouTube IPAs.

### Legend
⭐ — **Recommended.** It is actively maintained, free, includes pre-built IPAs, and has lots of tweaks.

---

### IPAs

⭐ [YouProEXTRA](https://github.com/mrdrvt99/YouProEXTRA)  
A YouTube IPA builder packed with customizable tweaks. Also known as Youtubemaxxing. Actively maintained, free, and includes pre-built IPAs.

⭐ [YTPlusM](https://github.com/Mark02-2012/YTPlusM)  
A fork of YouTube Plus (formerly YTLite). A flexible enhancer for YouTube featuring over a hundred customizable options. Actively maintained, free, and includes pre-built IPAs.

⭐ [YTKillerPlus](https://github.com/iKarwan/YTKillerPlus)  
[Repo](https://repo.ikghd.me/depictions/?p=me.ikghd.ytkplus)  
Actively maintained, free, and includes pre-built IPAs.

⭐ [YTLiteDiarrhea](https://github.com/diarrhea3/YTLiteDiarrhea)  
A fork of YouTube Plus (formerly YTLite). A flexible enhancer for YouTube featuring over a hundred customizable options. Free and includes pre-built IPAs.

[uYouEnhanced](https://github.com/arichornlover/uYouEnhanced)  
An expanded version of uYouPlus. Not actively maintained, free, and requires building the IPA yourself.

[YTLitePlusEXTRA](https://github.com/Tonwalter888/YTLitePlusEXTRA)  
A fork of YouTube Plus (formerly YTLite) that adds more tweaks similar to YTLitePlus. Actively maintained, free, and requires building the IPA yourself.

[YouMod](https://github.com/Tonwalter888/YouMod)  
Seeks to recreate features from YTLite as much as possible. Free and requires building the IPA yourself.

[YouTube Plus (formerly YTLite)](https://github.com/dayanch96/YTLite)  
Paid. The free version was discontinued, and it requires building the IPA yourself.
